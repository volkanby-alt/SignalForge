# SignalForge

**The Investment Intelligence Platform**

SignalForge is an AI-powered investment intelligence platform designed to understand where global capital is flowing, why it is moving there, and how those changes may affect a personal investment portfolio.

The goal is not to build a simple stock price tracker. SignalForge will combine stocks, crypto, macro trends, political decisions, institutional capital flows, company news, financial data, and AI analysis into one long-term investment research system.

> Haberi degil, sermaye akisini takip et.

---

## Core Vision

SignalForge aims to answer three questions for every important market development:

1. **Why did this happen?**
2. **Who may benefit or lose from it?**
3. **How can it affect the user's portfolio?**

The system should not only show news. It should interpret news and connect it to investment themes, sectors, companies, crypto assets, and the user's portfolio.

## Major Themes To Track

SignalForge will continuously monitor large investment themes such as:

- Artificial intelligence
- Semiconductors and chip supply chains
- Energy and electricity infrastructure
- Data centers
- Robotics and automation
- Space technologies
- Defense technologies
- Cybersecurity
- Quantum computing
- Real-world asset tokenization

**AI growth -> data center demand -> chip demand -> electricity demand -> energy and grid infrastructure investment**

## Planned Modules

### 1. Portfolio Tracker

- Live or delayed price tracking
- Position size
- Average cost
- Profit / loss
- Portfolio allocation
- Currency conversion support
- US stocks, BIST, funds, and crypto support

### 2. News Intelligence Engine

- Company-specific news
- Sector news
- Political and regulatory news
- Partnership and contract announcements
- Earnings and guidance updates
- Risk alerts
- AI-generated impact summary

### 3. Opportunity Detection

- Buy opportunity signals
- Profit-taking zones
- Strong pullback detection
- Overheated stock warnings
- Watchlist scoring

### 4. Earnings And Event Calendar

- Earnings dates
- Investor days
- Product launches
- Government contract announcements
- Token unlocks for crypto projects
- Macro events such as Fed decisions and CPI data

### 5. Emerging Technology Company Scanner

The system should continuously search for promising small and mid-cap technology companies.

Each company should be evaluated by:

- Business model
- Management quality
- Financial strength
- Revenue growth
- Cash position
- Capital allocation
- Partnerships
- Government contracts
- Competitive advantage
- Market size
- Dilution risk
- Debt risk
- Long-term potential

### 6. Crypto Research Engine

The crypto module should reduce a very large market into a small number of high-conviction projects.

Evaluation criteria:

- Real adoption
- Technology quality
- Developer activity
- Tokenomics
- Regulation risk
- Institutional interest
- Liquidity
- Security
- Ecosystem growth
- Long-term use case

Initial crypto categories:

- Bitcoin
- Ethereum
- RWA projects such as ONDO
- AI projects such as TAO / Bittensor
- DePIN projects
- Stablecoin infrastructure

### 7. Theme Map

SignalForge should connect themes to companies and assets.

Example:

- AI infrastructure -> NVDA, MU, ANET, data centers, energy infrastructure
- Space and defense -> RKLB, PLTR, government contracts
- RWA tokenization -> ONDO, institutional finance, regulation
- Robotics -> SYM, warehouse automation, logistics

### 8. Capital Flow Intelligence

The platform should try to understand where money is moving globally.

Sources to analyze over time:

- ETF flows
- Institutional filings
- Government spending
- Corporate capex plans
- M&A activity
- Venture funding
- Sector rotation
- Political priorities
- Major public statements
