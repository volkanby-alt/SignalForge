# Ideas

This document stores future ideas for SignalForge. No idea should be lost. Ideas can later become features, modules, or research tasks.

## Current Ideas

- Track where global money is moving.
- Connect political decisions to sectors and companies.
- Monitor BlackRock, Vanguard, Fidelity, Berkshire Hathaway, ARK Invest and other major institutions.
- Monitor US policy makers and major public statements.
- Track AI, chip, energy and data center investment flows.
- Identify small promising technology companies before they become widely known.
- Build a crypto research engine that filters many coins into a smaller high-quality watchlist.
- Provide 8-hour portfolio briefings.
- Build a living investment research platform that keeps expanding over time.

## Rule

SignalForge should never be considered finished. It should continuously grow with new ideas.
