# Vision

SignalForge is not a basic stock tracker. It is intended to become a long-term AI-powered investment intelligence platform.

## Mission

Understand where global capital is flowing, why it is flowing there, and how those flows may affect the user's portfolio.

## Core Questions

For every important market development, SignalForge should answer:

1. Why did this happen?
2. Who may benefit or lose?
3. How can this affect the user's portfolio?

## Long-Term Direction

SignalForge should combine:

- Stocks
- Crypto
- Funds
- Macro trends
- Political decisions
- Institutional capital flows
- Company news
- Earnings
- Government contracts
- AI analysis

The system should be built as a living platform that can grow for years.
