# Capital Flow Intelligence

SignalForge should try to understand where global money is moving.

## Goal

The platform should monitor capital flows and connect them to investment themes, sectors, companies, crypto assets, and the user's portfolio.

## Institutions To Track

- BlackRock
- Vanguard
- Fidelity
- Berkshire Hathaway
- ARK Invest
- Major hedge funds
- Major pension funds
- Sovereign wealth funds
- Large technology companies

## Signals To Monitor

- ETF flows
- Institutional filings
- Fund commentary
- Public interviews
- Sector allocation changes
- Large corporate capex plans
- M&A activity
- Venture funding
- Government spending
- Defense and infrastructure budgets

## Key Questions

- Where is money moving?
- Why is it moving there?
- Which sectors are gaining attention?
- Which sectors are being ignored?
- Which companies may benefit?
- How does this affect the user's portfolio?
